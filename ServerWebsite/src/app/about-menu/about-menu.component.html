About the Uberpower Server

If you are new to the Uberpower Server, then Welcome!

What is the Uberpower Server? I'm glad you asked! The Uberpower server is all about offering services to a small community of programmers (if you can call us that) and at the same time provide them with a platform where they can test out software, learn more about the Linux operating system, test out learned concepts in real life etc. Basically you decide what to do and when to do it.

This server is currently in its beta form (in other words: it is being developed!), so please be pacient if you find bugs or other inconveniences.

If you want to report something you can head over to the "contact" section of the website and report bugs, problems and everything you can think of. You can also give us feedback, so that we know on what to improve and where to focus on. Thank you very much for all your pacience. We hope you enjoy your time here.
